Our Services

Equipment/Vendor Leasing Advice

Guidance and Advice to find the most appropriate Lease or Rental Structure to meet corporate asset acquisition objectives

Funding/Capital Management/Private Equity

The matching of the most appropriate financing solution to client requirements that preserve or diversify existing sources of capital including the potential introduction of new equity or venture capital investors

Mergers and Aquistitions

Project Management of the end to end sale and purchase process including due diligence and post-acquisition integration. Services encompass matching buyers and sellers of companies and asset portfolios

Strategy Review, Development and Alignment

Assessment and Recommendation leading to the optimisation of strategy to align with dynamic business objectives. Crisis management and the development of turnaround plans.

Financial Partnership Selection

Advising, recommending and managing the critical decision process of matching end users, equipment manufacturers, suppliers and vendors with the most appropriate financial partner to meet corporate objectives and ensure future success.

Business Model Assessment

The selection of the most appropriate programme business model – Referral, Vendor Programme or Joint Venture together with the negotiation of the Co-operation Agreement where in-depth subject matter expertise is essential.

Investment Decision Analysis

Lease or Buy - Finance or Operating Lease – Fair Market Value lease – Long Term Rental – Hire Purchase – Sale and Lease Back - Rent to Buy – Pay for Use - Flex Leasing. Selecting the correct product from the myriad available together with the associated costs and benefits are essential issues to fully understand at the outset.

Asset Management

Guidance on optimum asset management strategies for acquisition and disposal of all asset classes including sale and leaseback of existing assets, access to second user markets, secure asset disposal and green recycling of end of life assets.
